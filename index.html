Research proposal for MSc Islamic Finance

A Shariah-Governed MCDM Readiness Framework for Smart Infrastructure Sukuk

The proposal develops SC-SIIRS, a Shariah-Compliant Smart Infrastructure Investment Readiness Score. It integrates financial viability, Maqasid al-Shariah alignment, ESG contribution, smart infrastructure performance, Shariah governance and risk exposure into one auditable pre-screening architecture for sukuk candidates.

The model is deliberately advisory. It organizes evidence and exposes trade-offs before formal sukuk structuring; it does not issue fatwas, price sukuk, certify Shariah compliance or override human governance.
Primary methodAHP expert weighting + TOPSIS scenario ranking.
RobustnessMEREC and COPRAS to test ranking stability.
ValidationExpert interviews for Shariah-governance fit and usability.
Decision momentPre-structuring stage before sukuk approval.

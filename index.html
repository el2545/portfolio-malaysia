Research anchor

SC-SIIRS is the main intellectual product.

The portfolio is built around one defensible research idea: a Shariah-governed readiness framework for smart infrastructure sukuk.

Research proposal for MSc Islamic Finance

A Shariah-Governed MCDM Readiness Framework for Smart Infrastructure Sukuk

The proposed SC-SIIRS framework integrates financial viability, Maqasid al-Shariah alignment, ESG contribution, smart infrastructure performance, Shariah governance and risk exposure into an auditable pre-screening architecture for sukuk candidates.

The model is advisory. It organizes evidence and exposes trade-offs before formal sukuk structuring; it does not issue fatwas, price sukuk, certify Shariah compliance or override human governance.
Primary methodAHP expert weighting + TOPSIS scenario ranking.
RobustnessMEREC and COPRAS test ranking stability.
ValidationExpert interviews test Shariah-governance fit and usability.
Decision momentPre-structuring stage before sukuk approval.
